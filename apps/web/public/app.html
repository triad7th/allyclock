AllyClock

The clock that fills your screen. Your cities, at a glance.

  • Fullscreen face: big, thin, beautiful digits with seconds, weekday, and time zone.
  • World Cards face: the cities you care about, with flags, dates, and offsets.
  • Everything adjusts live: sizes, cities, and row breaks, with no save buttons.
  • Controls fade away on their own, leaving just the time.
  • Made for iPhone and iPad in landscape, and for any browser.
