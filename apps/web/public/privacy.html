Privacy Policy

Effective July 9, 2026

AllyClock does not collect, store, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

What the app stores

Your preferences (selected clock face, size adjustments, and your list of world-clock cities) are saved only on your device so the app remembers them between launches. They never leave your device.

Network access

The iOS app works entirely offline. The web version at allyclock.netlify.app sets no tracking cookies; images you add to the Daily Schedule face are stored in your browser and are never uploaded.
