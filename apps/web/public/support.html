AllyClock Support

AllyClock is a fullscreen clock for iPhone, iPad, and the web: a big, beautiful time display and a world clock with the cities you care about.

Get help

Found a bug, have a feature request, or need a hand? Email wonseok.lee80@gmail.com and include your device model and iOS version if it's about the app.

Tips

  • Tap anywhere to reveal the controls; they fade away on their own.
  • The clock button switches faces: Fullscreen or World Cards.
  • The sliders button resizes the time and date independently.
  • The gear opens Settings: toggle seconds, weekday, time zone and flag on the Fullscreen face, or manage your world-clock cities and row breaks on World Cards.
  • Everything applies live; close a panel any way you like and your changes stick.
